# Product Requirements

## MVP Features (Must-Haves)
1. **Functional Mahjong Solitaire**
    - High contrast, high-quality graphics for easy viewing (especially for older gamers).
    - Match-2 logic utilizing a 4-slot bar (giving exactly 1 mistake buffer).
2. **Story-Driven Levels**
    - Saving the king from different problems on every level.
    - Tie match progress to boss/disaster progression.
3. **Functional Tutorial**
    - Extremely simple, intuitive onboarding so users don't get confused.

## Target Audience
- Casual gamers and seniors looking for a relaxing time-killer.
- They aren't highly tech-savvy and appreciate simple, understandable elements.

## Design aesthetic
- **Vibe:** Calming, peaceful, nature, puzzle.
- **Visuals:** Wooden bamboo borders with gold ingot accents, flat design, clean vector style.

## Key Success Metrics (First 30 days)
- DAU (Daily Active Users): Thousands within the first week
- App Rating: 4.5+ stars
- MRR: $5k/day and growing (Ethical Ads and minor IAPs)

## NOT in MVP
- World-Building System (Like Gardenscapes). This is saved for later to ensure 2 week launch.
